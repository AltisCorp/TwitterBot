TwitterBot

What is TwitterBot?

     TwitterBot is a computer program that shows you local Tweets about the Miami transportation system. Want to know which cabs are better than others? How about the ideal time to take the metro? We can help you with that.

Who is TwitterBot?

     TwitterBot is, of course, the logical precursor to Skynet from the Terminator movies. The TwitterBot Team is only a small part of Code for Miami, a volunteer group dedicated to bettering the city through creating beneficial code. We call it: Civic Hacking. TwitterBot was created by Kevin Hernandez.

Got a wrench?

     How about a metaphorical wrench? We are always looking for help in making our cute, little bot tick better. Swing on over to our Github to see how he works.
